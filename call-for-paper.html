Call for Papers

Prospective authors are encouraged to submit original and unpublished research papers (not under review elsewhere) following the Springer paper template via Microsoft CMT for ICATFS-2026. Submissions must be plagiarism-free and written in English.

The Microsoft CMT service was used for managing the peer-review process. Microsoft provided this service free of cost.
